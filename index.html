Cash Advance

Design lead

Cash advances have a reputation for being confusing and predatory. OnePay set out to change that. I led the design of OnePay's first-ever cash advance product, building an end-to-end experience that prioritized clarity and trust over financial jargon. At launch, over 80% of eligible users took an advance, and the feature continues to grow to this day.
